Basic Details and Information about this excursion.

＄ Price:

  • $ 65 / Person
  • Additional charge for Superman experience.

📍 Location:

  • Starting location is less than 15 minutes from Jaco.
  • Transportation is included (Jaco, Hermosa & Los Sueños).

⌚ Start Times:

  • Scheduled start times are between 8am and 3pm and run every hour on the hour.
  • Pick up from your house or condo is 20-30 minutes prior to your scheduled tour start time.
  • Duration of this tour is approximately 2 hours.

🎒 What to Bring:

  • Wear running shoes or strapped sandals. Do Not wear flip flops or they could fall off!
  • Comfortable jeans or shorts, with a t-shirt. You will be wearing a harness over your clothes.
  • Bug spray and or sun block if you feel these are necessary.
  • If you bring a camera or your phone, it is recommended that you use a wrist strap to secure it from falling.
  • A photo CD or USB drive will be available for purchase, with pictures of your group taken by the in-house photographer.
  • A few bucks to tip the guides.

Orientation, Equipment, Safety Instructions... and a Ride to the Top...

The first step after arriving and checking in, is to be fitted with the necessary gear and equipment. You will be supplied with gloves, a helmet and a harness which will be adjusted and checked by the staff. After the safety instructions and gear fitting, the guides and instructors will give you all the explanations needed to stay safe and comfortable during the tour.
