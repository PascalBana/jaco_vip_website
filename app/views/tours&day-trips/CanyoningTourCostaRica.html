WATERFALL CANYONING
RAPPELING + ZIPLINE

LOCATION :

15 Minutes drive from Jaco. After safety instructions, there is a 20 minute hike to the first zipline...

TOUR TIMES:
• 7:00am to 11:30am or
• 1:00pm to 5:30pm

WHAT TO BRING :
• Keep in mind: Your shoes and clothes will get wet!!
• Wear running shoes, strapped sandals or aqua shoes with soles that grip. Do not wear flip flops.
• Comfortable swimsuit or shorts.
• Camera in a waterproof bag.
• A few bucks [tips for guides].
• Insect repellent.
•••

ONLY 15 MINUTES DRIVE FROM JACO:
Once you arrive to the base camp... You will be outfitted with latest and safest gear from Black Diamond in the USA. After being instructed on the safe and proper use of the equipment, you will begin our hike into the canyon (approx 20 minutes). Your guide will stop along the way to point out anything interesting that might be seen along the way. White Face Monkeys, Toucans and Scarlet Macaws are common in the area.

THE TOTAL EXPERIENCE:
After arriving to the top, you will zipline over of a beautiful 200’ waterfall... and that is the first step!! After that, you will begin your descent down through the waterfalls.... Canyoning / Rappel down the face of 4 waterfalls that range in height from 60 to 90 feet!!

You will not be lowered down, YOU will do it all on your own! At all times you will be “safe and backed up” by your guides!

A short hike further and we will then “ZIP-OUT” on 2 Canopy Ziplines. The longest is over 2000 feet! We will then enjoy a tropical fruit snack and a beverage before returning to your house...
