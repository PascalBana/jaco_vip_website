LOS SUEÑOS RESORT COMMUNITY IS NESTLED IN A TROPICAL PARADISE :

Los Sueños Resort and Marina is located at Herradura Bay on the Central Pacific Coast of Costa Rica. Only 15 minutes from the nearby beach town of Jaco Beach and a two-hour scenic drive from Juan Santamaría International Airport in the capital city of San José, Costa Rica.

ABOUT THE RESORT :

Los Sueños Resort and Marina is a 1,100-acre master planned destination resort community. Los Sueños encompasses a private 600-acre rain-forest reserve; a 201-room five star Marriott hotel; over 500 luxury residences, including single-family homes, lots, condominiums and lavish villas; a full service 200-slip international marina; an 18-hole championship golf course, and much more.

THE FISHING CAPITAL OF COSTA RICA :

Los Sueños is internationally known as the Fishing Capital of Costa Rica. There are more trophy fish caught here year round than any other destination in Costa Rica. Whether you choose to fish Off shore for trophy Billfish or Near shore for Giant Rooster fish, or Snapper and Grouper, you'll find your best chances of Hooking Up by fishing with us out of Los Sueños Resort & Marina.
