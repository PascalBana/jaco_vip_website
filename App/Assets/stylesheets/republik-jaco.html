Republik Lounge is the Newest and Hottest Night Club in Jaco Beach.

A lot has changed in Jaco since Covid shut down social gatherings and tourism for more than a year. Some bars and restaurants survived, many closed their doors, and a few changed location... Republik Lounge changed location.

New Location.

Now located within the newly renovated Blue Marlin Hotel on Calle Cocal right in the center of town. Tucked away towards the back of the property, the club cannot be seen from the street which ads a layer of mystique and privacy.

Built From the Ground Up.

Built from the ground up (literally, nothing but gravel was there before) everything was constructed from scratch on the premises. Everything is brand new, and designed to offer the best service and experience for the clients.

The Next Level.

Republik Lounge has been a staple in the Jaco nightlife scene for many years. The nightclub has built a reputation for having a great party vibe and open to all kinds of groups and people. From locals to tourists everyone is welcome. The new location stepped it up to the next level.
