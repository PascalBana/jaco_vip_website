Casa Dulce is a spacious beach house

This villa has approx 4300 sq ft. of living space and beautiful beach frontage, on a large lot in the quiet area of Jaco's South Beach. Fully air conditioned, high ceilings, and airy interiors make it very fresh. Enjoy sunsets by the pool, surf, have a bbq and gaze at the stars. Right next door to another party friendly vacation house Dulce Grande 16 bedroom beachfront. The Main House includes living room area with sofas, big TV, large open concept kitchen ideal for chef service, a pool table than can be converted to a dining table. Upstairs Mater Bedroom has a King bed and private bathroom. The second bedroom has 2 Double beds and also has access to the balcony. The third bedroom has 2 Queen size beds and a half bathroom. These two bedrooms share a full bathroom across the hallway.

Adjacent to the main house entrance is another structure with outside access which houses a total of 6 bedrooms. The first floor has 3 bedrooms, The first bedroom has its own private bathroom. Bedrooms 2 and 3 share a jack and jill bathroom. There are 3 bedrooms on the second floor, each with their own full bathroom. Each of these 6 bedrooms has a door which lead to direct outside access to get to the main house.

This house is a good option for your Costa Rica bachelor party corporate retreat or family vacation reunion.
