DESCRIPTION:

Casa Cortes is a perfect vacation house for large groups, bachelor and bachelorette parties, large families, or multiple couples who would like to spend their vacation in a tropical paradise... Located on the beach, this home has seven bedrooms. The master suite accommodating a king-size bed. Each bedroom is equipped with A/C. The main indoor living room area and open kitchen are well furnished and equipped with comfortable sofas, kitchen essentials, cable TV and wireless internet.

DESIGNED FOR RELAXING & ENTERTAINING in Jaco Beach Style... The house is located directly on Jaco South Beach at the end of a street that runs along the beach, offering great privacy for lounging beachfront in the front of the house or partying by the pool in the back.
