It's Hot in Jaco! The Wild Side of Costa Rica Nightlife.

Jaco Beach is the epi-center of Costa Rica's nightlife. The party scene is regularly expanding with new dance clubs, beach bars and trendy restaurants offering live music or DJ sets. Back in the day, Jaco was a sleepy surfer town with dirt roads and rice & beans restaurants.. but in the last 12 years Jaco Beach has grown into having the hottest nightlife, and the undisputed reputation as party capital of Costa Rica!

VIP Guide to the Best Party in Jaco Beach.

The nightlife in Jaco is off the charts. Tourists vacation in Jaco from all over the world, as well as many locals from the capital city of San Jose, who come to enjoy the beach and of course the clubs, bars and party scene. It is common to see groups of friends on a Costa Rica bachelor party or bachelorette party, corporate retreats and family vacations. The Jaco VIP Concierge Team will escort you to all the best spots in town.

The most popular club for VIP tables and bottle service is Republik Lounge in the center of Jaco. It is located inside of The Blue Marlin Hotel and boasts the best sound system and light show.

The entire club was recently built (summer 2021) from the ground up. Everything is brand new and well laid out with 2 bars, plenty of vip tables and a dance floor in the center. The bathrooms are large, clean and lit with the latest in LED lighting. DJs come to Jaco from all over the country just to play at this club.

The most famous (or maybe notorious) nightlife in Costa Rica is at the Cocal Hotel and Casino which is also on the beach in the center of Jaco. Aside from being a popular casino and hotel, the outdoor pool bar area (aka Frog's Bar) is party central and sometimes has live latin music. The party usualy gets started pretty early and is not for the faint at heart.

There are some fantastic beach bars in Jaco with ocean and sunset views. These include Koko which is right in the center of town, and The Point which is a couple of blocks to the south. These restaurant bars are very popular among tourists and locals... and for good reason! They have great food and drinks, cold beer and good vibes all day and evening.

If you are looking to catch some live music... The Green Room is a popular urban oasis in the center of town with artisan food & drinks, local craft beer and live music every night of the week. A block down main street and you will find Jaco Bar which is a small but popular venue with AC, great food and live music on many nights.

In Conclusion...

Jaco Beach has the best Nightlife in Costa Rica.

There are some good discos/casinos/clubs in San Jose, but they are far apart from each other, and in some cases sketchy neighborhoods.

Tamarindo is a nice beach town with some good beach bars and restaurants. It is far from any airport and a small town with far less amenities.

Jaco Beach is a vacation destination that caters to both international tourists and local weekenders looking for tropical paradise with many daytime activities and vibrant nightlife. It is less than an hour and a half from the airport, and loaded with pretty much everything you could possibly want while on vacation in Costa Rica.
